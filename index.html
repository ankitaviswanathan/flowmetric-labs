About Flowmetric Labs

Built by operations people who got tired of guessing.

Flowmetric Labs was founded in 2022 by a team of operations leaders and applied ML engineers who spent years rebuilding the same dashboards in spreadsheets, BI tools, and ticketing systems — and still missing the problems that mattered.

We believed operations teams deserved the same quality of observability that engineering teams have had for a decade: live maps, clear signals, and alerts that tell you what to do before things break. So we built it.

Today, Flowmetric Labs helps mid-market B2B companies across SaaS and services run tighter onboarding, faster support, and more predictable revenue — without adding headcount.
